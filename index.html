Welcome to Adaptive Able Computing LLC

With over 30 years of professional IT experience and lived experience as a blind professional, I provide practical, real-world technology training grounded in both technical expertise and accessibility insight.

Adaptive Able Computing LLC provides adaptive computer and technology training for blind and low-vision adults and students in northern Indiana. With over 30 years of IT experience and lived experience as a blind professional, the business focuses on building independence, confidence, and real-world technology skills.

Services emphasize accessible technology for education, employment, and daily life. Most training is provided remotely, with in-person instruction available when needed.
