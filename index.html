Welcome to Access Able Computing LLC

Access Able Computing LLC provides adaptive computer and technology training for blind and low-vision adults and students in northern Indiana. With over 30 years of IT experience and lived experience as a blind professional, the business focuses on building independence, confidence, and real-world technology skills.

Services emphasize accessible technology for education, employment, and daily life. Most training is provided remotely, with in-person instruction available when needed.
