Services

Adaptive Technology & Windows Support Services

Access Able Computing LLC provides adaptive computer and technology training for blind and low-vision adults, transition-age youth, and K–12 students across northern Indiana.

Training is available through one-on-one sessions, small-group instruction, remote learning, and school-based support. Instruction is structured, practical, and individualized, drawing on more than 30 years of IT experience and lived experience as a blind professional.

  • Screen reader training (JAWS and NVDA)
  • Magnification and low-vision tools
  • Windows support and training for blind, low-vision, and sighted users
  • Accessible use of Windows applications and system settings
  • iPhone and iPad accessibility (VoiceOver)
  • Software, workplace, and productivity accessibility
  • Real-world technology skills for school, work, and daily life

Access Able Computing works in partnership with schools, rehabilitation agencies, blindness organizations, and accessibility-focused nonprofits to support independence, confidence, and opportunity through accessible technology.
